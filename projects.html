Machine Learning in Genomic Data Analysis

Developing novel neural network architectures to interpret large-scale sequencing data for early disease detection, specifically targeting rare genetic markers in population datasets.

Key Objectives:

  • Optimize clustering algorithms for high-dimensional data.
  • Reduce computational overhead by 30% on standard hardware.
  • Integrate findings with public health databases.

Quantum Error Correction Protocols

Investigation into surface codes and topological quantum computing methods to stabilize qubits against environmental noise, paving the way for scalable quantum processors.

Key Objectives:

  • Simulate noise models in superconducting circuits.
  • Develop a new decoder for the toric code.
